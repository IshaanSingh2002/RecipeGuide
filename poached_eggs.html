RECIPE GUIDE


Poached Eggs Italian Twisted Style

Ingredients




4 eggs
2 tbsp vinegar
6 slices prosciutto ham
2 large slices sour dough bread
8 slices mozzarella cheese
2 cups spinach leaves
10 cherry tomatoes
A few fresh chives
Olive oil
Salt and pepper

Steps Involved

Step 1
Place a large pot of water on high heat and
bring to a simmer. Add the vinegar.

Step 2
Place a griddle pan on high heat. Add the
bread slices and char grill each side. Alternatively,
simply use a toaster. Coat with a drizzle of
olive oil once toasted.

Step 3
Crack the eggs into individual cups and set aside.

Step 4
Place the prosciutto in a dry pan on medium heat
and cook until slightly crispy, flip over and cook
other side. You may need to cook 3 at a time.

Step 5
Slowly and very gently, pour in the eggs into
the simmering water. It should take 3 Â½ to 4
minutes to cook for runny yolks.

Step 6
Place the mozzarella cheese slices in a non stick
pan on medium heat, and cook until melted and
beginning to brown underneath.

Step 7
The eggs should be cooked, remove from water
using a slotted spoon and place over some
absorbent kitchen paper.
