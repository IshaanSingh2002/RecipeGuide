RECIPE GUIDE


Pesto Pasta

Ingredients




1 large chicken breast
8 Swiss brown mushrooms (small)
6 Sundried tomatoes
2 garlic cloves
Â½ tsp chili flakes (or to your taste)
1 cup chicken stock
2 tbsp pesto (buy it or see my recipe)
1 cup heavy cream
1 cup baby spinach
2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
7oz - 200g Fresh Tagliolini pasta
3 tbsp olive oil
Salt and pepper

Steps Involved

Step 1
Place a large pot of water on high heat for your pasta.
Add salt once the water boils.

Step 2
Slice the mushrooms medium thick. Chop the sundried
tomatoes, chop the spinach. Keep aside.

Step 3
Slice the chicken breast into thin slices width wise.

Step 4
Place a frying pan on medium heat with 2 tbsp of
olive oil and brown all the chicken pieces evenly.
Set aside.

Step 5
To the same pan, add 1 tbsp olive oil plus the sliced
mushrooms. Cook until brown. Add garlic cloves
minced or finely chopped. Add the sundried
tomatoes, chilli flakes and chicken stock. Return the
chicken to the pan. Season with salt and pepper,
add the pesto and cream. Leave on medium heat
until sauce has reduced to a thick consistency.
Stir occasionally.

Step 6
Serve pasta on hot plates, scoop up any chicken
pieces and place on top. Spoon on any sauce from
the pan. Top with a little grated Parmesan cheese.
