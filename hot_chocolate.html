RECIPE GUIDE


Italian Hot Chocolate

Ingredients




2½ cups Milk
2-3 tablespoons Sugar
1 tablespoon (8g) Cocoa powder
2 oz (60g) Dark quality chocolate (70%)
1 tablespoons Cornstarch
Pinch salt
Whipped cream + chocolate shavings

Steps Involved

Step 1
In a small mixing bowl whisk ¼ cup of the milk
and cornstarch until smooth, set aside.

Step 2
Pour the rest of the milk into a saucepan.
Add the sifted cocoa powder, sugar and pinch
of salt. Turn on heat to medium and whisk
ingredients. Leave to heat until it barely
simmers. Do not boil.

Step 3
Meanwhile whip some cream for later.

Step 4
Once hot chocolate simmers, add the corn
starch mixture and whisk well over high heat
until thick. Turn off heat and add the
chocolate pieces. Whisk continuously for
approximately 3 minutes. Serve immediately.
